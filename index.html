ENERGY CLOCK

This project was started as part of a cooperation between the energy company Fortum, and the KTH Stockholm. Specifically the Visualization course tought by Mario Romero. The goal is creating awareness of energy consumption among the general population.

The ENERGY CLOCK visualizes simulated energy consumption data in an average household to create this awareness. By touching the bars, the user can change between different visual styles. The different types of energy can be enabled and displayed by touching their respective icons. Finally when touching the time, the user can explore the energy consumption history. This visualization is best displayed on an iPad and was created for this type of device.
